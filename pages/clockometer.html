Clockometer

Want to experience time instead of just measuring it? Love cars? Clockometer is for you. It's your ultimate timepiece.

Clockometer doesn't just measure time, it enables you to feel it. It helps you understand the value of time and be in control of it. Every second of your learning life becomes absolutely exciting!

Clockometer features a fully capable ESP32 SoC microcontroller at its heart. When you build your Clockometer, you'll utilize many features of the ESP32 as an advanced system-on-a-chip microcontroller. Clockometer is a fast teacher and regardless of age or skill level, it brings you up to speed. It teaches you 3D printing, electronics, WiFi networking, API development, firmware development, and how to use servo motors found in robotics projects.

Additionally, you will learn how to connect your microcontroller to the internet to get the accurate time from precise atomic clocks online, keeping your timekeeping device at the edge of time.

The design of Clockometer is modular and open-source, allowing you to copy, modify, and update it for your personal use. In our more advanced content, we delve even deeper into the intricate details of circuitry, software development, and hardware design. As we make changes and updates, we'll guide you through the rationale behind certain design decisions, ensuring a deeper understanding and even more enjoyment throughout the learning process.
