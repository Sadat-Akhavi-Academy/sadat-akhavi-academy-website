Alpha Bike X

Alpha Bike X is an exceptional teacher for anyone, regardless of age or skill level. It's a remote-controlled bike that teaches you electronics, 3D printing, prototyping, and coding, all through enjoyable and interactive experiences.

You build it from the ground up, gaining a deep understanding of how each component works, its placement, and how it can be repaired or upgraded. Soldering the components of the main controller board yourself is not only a valuable learning experience but also incredibly enjoyable.

Afterwards, you'll assemble and program electronic components like microcontrollers (MCU), RF communication, Servo motors, and more onto the board, seamlessly integrating them with the 3D-printed parts to bring the bike to life.

All components of the bike are modular and the designs are open-source, allowing you to copy, modify, and update them for your personal use.

In our more advanced content, we delve even deeper into the intricate details of circuitry, software development, and hardware design. As we make changes and updates, we'll guide you through the rationale behind certain design decisions, ensuring a deeper understanding and even more enjoyment throughout the learning process.
