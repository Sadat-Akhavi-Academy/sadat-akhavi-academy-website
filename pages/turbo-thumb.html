Turbo Thumb

Turbo Thumb gives you complete control over your RC army with just one controller. It features a Device-Selector for targeting specific devices and a Broadcast mode for sending commands to all devices at once.

Equipped with the powerful NRF24L01 RF module, Turbo Thumb ensures your devices stay connected up to 1km. It operates at 2.4GHz with an external antenna for reliable performance.

Turbo Thumb has a redundant power supply design with two internal batteries and two external ports for extended use. Powered by the Atmega 2560 Microcontroller, it offers extensive IO ports for customization.

The open-source design allows for continuous updates and customization. We provide detailed content on circuitry, software development, and hardware design, ensuring you stay updated and enjoy the learning process.
