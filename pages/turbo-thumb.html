Turbo Thumb

Turbo Thumb is our shining beacon on the horizon! With just one controller, you have complete control over all your RC Army! It's a one-time investment that pays off every time.

Turbo Thumb's Device-Selector feature gives you the power to choose the target device you want to control among your fleet. Additionally, it has a Broadcast-mode which allows you to send one command to all your devices at once and mobilize your entire fleet!

With Turbo Thumb's powerful NRF24L01 RF module, your devices are never out of range. Operating at 2.4GHz with an external antenna, you can be assured of great reliability and throughput up to 1km.

Turbo Thumb boasts a highly redundant power supply design, utilizing 2x redundant primary and secondary internal batteries for extended use, and 2x external ports for additional battery support on those days when you need extra power to extend the fun.

Featuring a fully capable and highly powerful Atmega 2560 Microcontroller at its core, Turbo Thumb provides a high level of control and customization with its extensive IO ports, enabling you to add new features and customize it to suit your needs.

Since Turbo Thumb's design is open-source and customizable, we will share new updates to the design and code, keeping your Turbo Thumb at the cutting edge of the latest technology and never out of fashion.

In our more advanced content, we delve even deeper into the intricate details of circuitry, software development, and hardware design. As we make changes and updates, we'll guide you through the rationale behind certain design decisions, ensuring a deeper understanding and even more enjoyment throughout the learning process.
