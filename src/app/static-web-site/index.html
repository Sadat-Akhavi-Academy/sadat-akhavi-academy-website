ABOUT US

Education is where our heart is.

We are a Melbourne-based startup focusing on education through play. We help youngsters learn and master new skills in areas such as Robotics, Electronics, Programming, 3D Printing, and 3D Design. We believe that toys are not just toys; they are a means for youngsters to express themselves and change their world. That's why all our designs are practical; youngsters can see their hard work and creations come to life and be used in meaningful ways. They can understand how they can impact the world from a very young age. We help them build their self-confidence and add value to their lives. Our designs cater to all age ranges, from young kids who can assemble and learn from them to parents who assemble and learn alongside their children, spending quality time together.
